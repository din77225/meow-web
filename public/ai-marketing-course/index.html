curriculum

What You'll Learn

Six modules across six weeks. Each builds on the last.

Week 1

Brand Foundation

Extract your brand voice from existing materials. Build a reusable brand doc, a visual brand book with AI-generated design, and project context that makes all future AI output consistent.

2.5 hours brand voice doc + brand book + copy
Week 2

Image Generation

Learn which models to use for which job. Master prompting, reference images, style control, multi-element scenes, and batch generation with quality control.

3 hours 5 campaign images + batch run
Week 3

Design Applications

Turn AI images into real marketing materials. Create editable posters in Canva, slide decks, and handoff-ready landing page mockups.

2.5 hours poster + landing page mockup
Week 4

Video Generation

Produce a short-form video ad using AI. Cover video prompting, model selection, the image-to-commercial workflow, plus lip sync and music tools.

3 hours 1 video ad
Week 5

Content Intelligence

Reverse-engineer any brand's content strategy using AI that actually watches videos. Grab data fast, synthesize in NotebookLM, and turn insights into a content calendar and script outline for your own brand.

2.5 hours content strategy + calendar draft
capstone
Week 6

Portfolio Review + Q&A

Compile your weekly deliverables into a complete campaign portfolio. Present your work, get direct feedback from Mia, and open Q&A on anything from the course.

2.5 hours campaign portfolio + feedback
